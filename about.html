About Our Team

Cyber Guard Team is a group of students learning cybersecurity together. We are focused on simple web security, safe practices, and helping others understand why protection matters.

Our mission

We want to show that even a basic website can communicate a professional team identity while remaining easy to navigate on any device.

  • Share cybersecurity knowledge in plain language.
  • Present services clearly and simply.
  • Build a site that works on phones and desktops.

Team values

Simple, honest, and practical. These values guide our design choices and the way we create each page.
